About me

_____

My name is Brooke Hargrove, a former fashion assistant and publicist turned UX designer, currently based in New York City. I was born in Michigan, but grew up abroad in Germany, Thailand and the UAE. I graduated with a liberal arts degree in Anthropology from Mount Holyoke College with an affinity for studying and conducting field research to further understand human behavior and how it evolves over time across various cultures.

Since graduating, I’ve worked at leading fashion companies and the exposure has fine-tuned my eye for beautifully crafted design. In an effort to combine my love for fashion and design and the social science of Anthropology, I was naturally drawn to UX design. I love finding problems, and collaboratively creating elegant solutions for an effortless, intuitive experience.

Other than UX, I’m an avid traveler, I love reading, photography, musicals, baking and frequenting museums.
